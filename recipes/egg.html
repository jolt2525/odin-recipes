How to make perfect scrambled eggs

here you are going to learn one of the best and most simple ways to create a delicious breakfast dish.

What you will need

  • Eggs (amount is up to you)
  • butter
  • optional bread for eggs on toast
  • optional salt, pepper and chives

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into pan or bowl/container
  2. whisk the eggs untill mixed
  3. cook on a low heat
  4. stire occasionaly when the eggs begin to cook on the bottom
  5. if eggs start to cook too quickly remove from the heat
  6. when the eggs are only partly liquid put the toast on
  7. remove the eggs from heat just after they have just turned from a liquid to a paste
  8. now a pinch of salt and pepper
  9. place the scrambled eggs on a thinly buttered slice of toast
  10. sprinkly the chives ontop of the food
